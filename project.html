In the last year of high-school in 2022/2023, I along with two other teammates, made a project on the instructions of "Demcon". This project had as goal to design a small assignment for all students from the ages 9 up to and untill 14, where they get to know Demcon, the world of mechatronics, and coding. We decided on making a robot-kit for a self-driving robot that could follow a line all by itself, along with a manual. With this manual the students would be able to assemble and code the robot-kit. The goals were reached and the designed robot was able to follow a line all by itself and a manual was made.
In 2021/2022, I along with three students, made a project for "CTT"". This project had as goal too prevent damage on cranes and containers by using sensors. As crane operateors sometimes drop the containers too hard, the containers along with the crane might get damaged, and will have to be serviced sooner. To reduce damage and costs a system with two sensors, accelerometer and sound sensor, was designed to detect if the crane was coming down too fast. I the speed was too high a signal would be given to the crane operator to reduce it's speed. At the end of the project, the sensors worked, but a real system was not realised. But the concept did work.
